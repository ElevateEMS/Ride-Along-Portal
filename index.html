Ride Along Request Notice If you need help arranging a ride-along, fill out your request on the separate Ride Along Request tab — with as much notice as possible. We require at least one month's notice when scheduling ride-alongs; requests submitted with less notice may not be guaranteed a spot.

Before Your Ride-Alongs

  • Complete Modules 1–2 (including quizzes and exams)
  • Review the following skills videos: Patient Assessment and Vital Signs
  • Review the other skills videos in case opportunities arise to perform these skills
  • Obtain 5 sets of vital signs and upload documentation to JB Learning
  • Pass the Ride Along Readiness Quiz
