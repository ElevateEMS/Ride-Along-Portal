Ride Along Request Notice If you need help arranging a ride-along, fill out your request on the separate Ride Along Request tab — with as much notice as possible. We require at least one month's notice when scheduling ride-alongs; requests submitted with less notice may not be guaranteed a spot.

Before Your Ride-Alongs

  • Complete Modules 1–2 (including quizzes and exams)
  • Review the following skills videos: Patient Assessment and Vital Signs
  • Review the other skills videos in case opportunities arise to perform these skills
  • Obtain 5 sets of vital signs and upload documentation to JB Learning
  • Pass the Ride Along Readiness Quiz
  • Read the "Patient Contact Instructions" sheet before filling out your documents
Acknowledgment

Read and acknowledge the following statements and expectations. By signing below, you understand that this electronic signature is legally binding and carries the same effect as a handwritten signature.

  • I understand Elevate EMS is not liable for any injury, illness, exposure, or damages during my ride alongs.
  • I will follow all course and agency expectations and will not falsify documentation.
  • I cannot begin ride alongs until the following criteria are met: Modules 1 & 2 are complete, patient assessment and vital signs skills videos are viewed, Vital Signs Practice Assignment is completed, and the Ride Along Readiness Quiz is passed.
  • 10 patient contacts are required (a minimum of 5 real contacts are required prior to Academy Week).
  • All instructions and required paperwork are located in JB Learning. I must complete a Clinical Training Record and a Patient Contact Form for each patient contact.
